Welcome

Hello, my name is Alex Gertner and I am a freelance horn player in the New York and New Jersey area. I have a lot of experience playing a variety of different styles of music including orchestral, chamber music, jazz, and musicals. I also teach lessons privately to any age and skill level. Check out my website for information, recordings, and upcoming performances!
